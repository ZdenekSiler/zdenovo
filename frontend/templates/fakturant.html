Core Features

Invoice Editor

Full invoice form with line items, supplier/customer details, VAT rates, and variable symbols. Split-pane live preview updates as you type (400ms debounce). Auto-save every 1.5 seconds.

PDF Export

Pixel-perfect PDF generation via WeasyPrint. Three templates — modern, classic, minimal. Embeds your logo and signature as base64. Same Jinja2 templates render both the preview and the final PDF.

Dashboard

KPI cards for outstanding, overdue, monthly, and YTD revenue. 12-month bar chart (issued vs. paid), status donut, aging analysis by days overdue, and a recent invoices table. Powered by Chart.js.

ARES Lookup

Search the Czech business registry (ARES) by company name or tax ID. Auto-fills supplier and customer fields — name, address, DIČ, IČO. Async HTTP client, returns results in under a second.

Payment QR Codes

Generates Czech SPD-standard QR codes for bank transfers. Customers scan with their banking app to auto-fill the payment — account number, amount, variable symbol, all pre-populated.

Multi-User Auth

Email + password signup with Argon2id hashing. Signed session cookies, row-level data isolation (each user sees only their invoices). Optional email verification and password reset via Resend API.
